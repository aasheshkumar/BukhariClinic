ABOUT US

Welcome to Bukhari Veterinary Clinic

Animal Clinic of Dilsher and Wakash is here to help your animals live their best lives possible. Our teams of doctors, technicians, and support staff are highly trained and ready 12 hours a day, 7 days a week to provide advanced veterinary medical care for the thousands of patients referred to us each year at locations.

Our Specialty Centers function like a human clinic, meaning patients are referred to specialists by their primary care veterinarians and the emergency service is fully staffed and always ready for the unexpected.
