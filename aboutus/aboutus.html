ABOUT US

Bukhair Veterinary Clinic

BUKHARI VETERINARY CLINIC is a privately owned medical clinic for veterinary practices servicing Bhan Saeedabad, Sehwan Sharif and Sindh areas. Located in Bhan Saeedabad, Bukhair Veterinary Clinic perform medical and surgical procedures on animals such as Horses, Camels, Cows, Sheep, Dogs, Cats, Rabbits, Birds and any other animals. We continually invest in upgrading our equipment to provide your pet with the latest in veterinary healthcare. we provide all pets and animals with affordable professional service. We are committed to offering the very best in veterinary healthcare, aiming to set the standard for excellence by providing you and your pets with exceptional service. We pride ourselves on continuing veterinary healthcare education by requiring our team to attend major veterinary conferences each year,. All the products, treatments and services provided are up to date and of the highest level to suit our clients and patients circumstances.
